Supercop and hacker

Written by

 Nick Van Looveren and Thijs Verbelen

Nick.vanlooveren@immalle.be  / Thijs.verbelen@immalle.be

First scene: The hacker is sitting in a dark room. He’s hacking the system of the bank. He’s stealing millions of euros.

 

Second scene: The following morning, the crime is being shown on the news. When Supercop Tistie is having a free day, he realises that it’s about to end soon because he sees the breaking news. He’s back on duty. He grabs his phone and quickly searches the location of the criminal on snapchat. When he knows the criminal’s location, he jumps out of his seat, grabs his two guns, puts on his nice sunglasses and runs outside to his expensive Supercop go-car.

 

Third scene: Supercop Tistie rides away. When Tistie arrives, the hacker notices the supercop and quickly runs to the door to wait for him. The Supercop storms inside but when he does, the hacker pushes him aside and runs away. The Supercop starts the epic chase. After a long chase, Tistie succeeds in arresting the ruthless hacker.

 

Fourth scene: Supercop Tistie interrogates the hacker in the interrogation room.

 

Fifth scene: What happens next is a surprise.

 

 

FADE IN:

 

INT. DARK ROOM - NIGHT

 

The hacker is hacking the system of the bank. He’s stealing millions of euros.

 

HACKER

Yes! I’m in the system, now I’ll take all the money. HAHA my new account balance is 3 billion euros.

 

INT. LIVING ROOM - MORNING

 

Supercop Tistie is looking at the news and he wants to arrest the hacker. He grabs his phone and searches for the location of the hacker. He takes his guns and puts on his sunglasses. He runs to the Supercop go-car. Tistie rides away.

 

SUPERCOP TISTIE

God damn it, my free day! Can’t they do anything without me??? Pffff, I’ll go fix it.

EXT. And INT. STREET And HACKER’S HOME

 

Tistie arrives at the hacker’s home. The hacker notices the Supercop and runs to the door and waits for him. The cop runs inside but he gets pushed by the hacker. The hackers runs away and the cop starts the chase, Tistie arrests the hacker.

 

SUPERCOP TISTIE

Ahh...this must be his home. Let’s get him!.

 

HACKER

Oh shit! It’s the Supercop, he won’t get me! I’m going to run away when he’s inside.

 

SUPERCOP TISTIE

WOWW...this door is heavy. What the fuck! Hey get your hands up in the air! Okay then I’ll chase you then.

 

HACKER

You’ll never catch me alive!!!

 

SUPERCOP TISTIE

I’m getting closer!

 

HACKER

Shit!!

Aww...you’re heavy dude!

 

SUPERCOP TISTIE

Thanks!

 

INT. INTERROGATION ROOM - AFTERNOON

 

Supercop Tistie interrogates the hacker.

 

SUPERCOP TISTIE

Tell me, why did you stole the money?

 

HACKER

Why not?

 

SUPERCOP TISTIE

Tell me why!!!

 

HACKER

Okay, I wanted to spend it all on booze.

 

 

INT. LIVING ROOM - EVENING

 

:D Surprise :D
